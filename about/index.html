🌍 What This Site Is

This is a place where I share my thoughts, work, and learning notes.
It is not bound by the laws of any specific country, so it is, relatively speaking, free.
It started taking shape in the middle of 2025 and has gradually been organized by me and AI together.
Everything you see here—especially my works—has been made to the best of my ability.
Constrained by various limitations, it may not be perfect, and I would appreciate your understanding.
If one day I am gone, this place, together with my private photo albums, will be far more worthy of being my tombstone.

🧭 Personal Values

I am a beast wrapped in reason.

I do not long for morality. Morality often creates a questionable standard for dividing good from evil; when faced honestly, neither you nor I come out looking all that righteous.
I do not care much for the label of pathology. What you call pathological is often just difference.
So I prefer to begin with doubting myself, accepting carefully and rejecting carefully.
I try to restrain hostility, because hatred corrodes wisdom and conscience.
I try to take good care of the complexity of human nature, to understand the cost of freedom, and then to remain stubbornly committed to what is good.
I refuse to glorify suffering, but I admire everyone who fights desperately for something beautiful.
In the vast universe, I am only a temporary life form assembled from different elements. Dirty and ugly, incapable and ignorant. Brief encounters, eternal partings. Between coming and going, memory is almost all we have.
I hope to remain sensitive enough to experience life deeply.
Since it took so much luck to arrive on Earth, I would rather leave with as few regrets as possible. ✨

🔥 On Belief

  • I identify with liberalism and lean toward libertarian views of free will.
  • I do not like the doctrine of the mean; the wise are not obliged to preserve themselves.
  • Flaws are inevitable, but I am still willing to believe in human possibility.
  • I do not love parties, nations, or classes; I care more about real individuals. Even if we disagree, I will still try to respect you and empathize with you.
  • I am probably not a strict materialist. My deceased family members are the gods who shelter me.
  • Freedom, to me, means stepping outside every imposed label and becoming someone who cannot be easily defined.
